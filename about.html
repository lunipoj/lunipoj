About us

Brand History

Onyx was founded in 2017. We male the best backpacks, insisting on designing original products and striving for excellence. After many years of unremitting development, our products reached international recognition in various markets throughout the world.

Our manufacturing partner, Jamesay, has been in the business since 2016 manufacturing backpacks and 3c products. By partnering, we ensure a stable supply chain dedicated to the production of high-quality products.

Our goal is to create products that inspire users to live a passionate life. Explore and create your future with an array of products made for travel, business and study.
